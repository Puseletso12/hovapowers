Detailed information: Generators

What we offer

Our generator range covers silent canopy sets, open-frame units, mobile trailer-mounted generators and containerised power plants. We partner with reliable engine and alternator brands, and pair every set with SmartGen or Deep Sea Electronics (DSE) controllers, ATS / AMF panels and correctly rated changeover switches. Fuel tanks, bunded bases, extended-run tanks and remote monitoring are available on request.

Benefits of a properly installed generator

A correctly sized generator protects your equipment from voltage spikes, reduces downtime during load shedding, keeps refrigeration and IT loads online, and preserves revenue in retail, hospitality and industrial environments. Automatic Mains Failure panels start the set within seconds of a grid outage — no manual intervention, no lost production.

Industries we serve

We install generators for construction sites, telecommunications towers, data centres, shopping centres, hospitality venues, hospitals and clinics, mining operations, filling stations, farms and residential estates. Each environment has different load, noise and emissions requirements — we assess site conditions and design accordingly.

Common customer questions

How big a generator do I need? We measure your essential load and future growth before recommending a kVA rating. How loud is it? Our silent canopies typically operate at 65–75 dB at 7 m. How often should it be serviced? Every 250 running hours or six months, whichever comes first. Do you handle fuel? Yes — we supply bunded tanks and can arrange fuel management contracts.

Why choose Hova's Power Solutions

We combine electrical engineering, mechanical servicing and after-sales support under one roof. Every installation includes a load test, commissioning report, operator training and a maintenance schedule. Emergency callouts are available for existing service customers, and we stock consumables so your generator is back online fast.
